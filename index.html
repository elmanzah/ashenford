Markets Served

Two high-growth markets. One procurement strategy.

Hospitality and workforce housing share a defining trait: they never stop purchasing. Renovation cycles, brand conversions, new construction, and infrastructure-driven crew housing generate billions in recurring FF&E demand — and both depend on partners who deliver on time, on budget, and with minimal disruption.

Hospitality — Hotels & Resorts

An industry built on continuous renewal. Guest rooms turn over on a 5–7 year renovation cycle and mattresses on a 7–10 year replacement cycle, meaning every property purchases repeatedly across its life.

  • Guest room renovations & brand conversions
  • New construction & property openings
  • Mattress & bedding replacement programs
  • Public spaces, lobby & reception FF&E

Temporary Workforce Housing

America's infrastructure boom — data centers, energy projects, manufacturing, and large-scale development — is creating unprecedented demand for furnished accommodations that can stand up fast and perform under heavy use.

  • Data center & energy project crew housing
  • Extended-stay & corporate housing refreshes
  • Complete furniture & sleep-system packages
  • Multi-family & student housing developments

The Project Lifecycle

Seven core services. One seamless engagement.

Most FF&E projects fail in the seams — between the manufacturer, the freight company, and the installation crew. Ashenford removes the seams by owning every stage of the lifecycle through a single point of contact.

  1. Procurement & Strategic Sourcing

    Factory-direct sourcing across a curated network of domestic and international manufacturing partners — vendor management, product specification, budget optimization, and purchasing coordination through one trusted source.

  2. Design & Space Planning

    Interior design coordination, space planning, furniture layouts, and product selection that align each environment with operational goals, brand standards, and the guest experience.

  3. Furniture, Fixtures & Bedding Solutions

    Commercial-grade case goods, seating, mattresses, bedding systems, and support foundations engineered for hospitality and workforce environments — selected for durability, comfort, and long-term value.

  4. Décor, Finishing Elements & Soft Goods

    Lighting, artwork, mirrors, rugs, window treatments, hospitality linens, and textiles — the finishing details that bring a space to life and reflect each property's brand.

  5. Logistics & White-Glove Delivery

    Nationwide transportation, warehousing, inventory management, delivery scheduling, and room-by-room placement — coordinated so products arrive where they need to be, when they need to be, with minimal disruption.

  6. Installation & Project Execution

    Experienced installation teams handle assembly, placement, on-site coordination, supervision, and quality control — delivered safely, efficiently, and on schedule.

  7. Removal & Project Completion

    Existing furniture removal, debris clearing, site clean-up, punch-list completion, final walkthrough, and project closeout — because our commitment doesn't end when installation begins.
